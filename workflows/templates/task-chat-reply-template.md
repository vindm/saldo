# Reply templates for the Finkoper task chat

Proven phrasings for typical situations accumulate here. Before using a new one — get it approved by me.

## Principles

- Short, to the point, no fluff
- No emotion ("it's a pity", "unfortunately", "what a nightmare") — neutral tone
- No promises about deadlines we don't control
- If the reply is addressed to a client — use formal address, politely
- If to colleagues/the manager — a business tone, can be simpler

## Template 1: Received the task, started working

> Took the task into work. I need [list]. Requested from the client, deadline [date].

## Template 2: Requested data from the client

> Requested [what] from the client. Waiting for a reply by [date].

## Template 3: Received the data, getting to work

> Received [what]. Starting on [action]. Will be ready [when].

## Template 4: Task requires clarification from the supervisor/manager

> [Name], I need your check on [question]. Context: [briefly]. Question: [specific one]. Waiting for a reply to continue.

## Template 5: Task complete

> Done. [What was done]. [Where the results were placed / what was sent to the client]. Closing the task.

(templates are added as they accumulate)
