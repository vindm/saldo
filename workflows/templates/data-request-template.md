# Templates for requesting data from a client

Proven phrasings accumulate here. Before using a new one — get it approved by me.

## General request structure

Any request to a client should contain:

1. Greeting
2. Context — for which period, on which matter
3. A specific (numbered) list of what's needed
4. Format of delivery (PDF/Excel/photo)
5. Deadline or a soft phrasing of urgency
6. Where to send it (Finkoper / email / WhatsApp)

## Template 1: Request for the month's primary documents

> Hello, [name]!
>
> To close [month year], the following documents are needed:
>
> 1. Bank statements for [period] for all accounts
> 2. Cash register reports / Z-reports (if there's a cash register)
> 3. Contracts and acts with new counterparties (if any appeared)
> 4. Documents for receipts and expenses on a personal card for business transactions
>
> PDF or photo is most convenient. Send via Finkoper or email by [date].
>
> Thank you!

## Template 2: Request for supporting documents

> Hello, [name]!
>
> For [period / document / transaction] we need supporting documents:
>
> 1. [Contract / act / invoice / UPD with the counterparty "…", for the period …]
> 2. [Payment document — if the payment went through another account or in cash]
> 3. [If a refund/adjustment — the basis (letter, act, claim)]
>
> The documents are needed for [reason: posting into 1C / reconciliation with the bank / a reply to the supervisor / confirmation of a USN expense, etc.].
>
> PDF or photo is most convenient. Send via Finkoper or email by [date].

## Template 3: Request for an FTS reply

> Hello, [name]!
>
> The FTS [inspectorate number] has sent a demand dated [date] for [submission of documents / clarifications] regarding [period / topic]. The reply deadline is [date].
>
> To prepare a correct reply, we need:
>
> 1. [Specific document 1 — explain why]
> 2. [Specific document 2]
> 3. [Clarifications on the substance of the transaction / counterparty, if the demand is for clarifications]
>
> Documents in PDF, clarifications — as text in the chat. Deadline: by [a date 1-2 days before the FTS reply deadline, to have time to prepare the reply].
>
> This is important: failure to submit documents / clarifications on time carries a penalty of 5,000 RUB (clause 1, Art. 129.1 of the Tax Code for clarifications; Art. 126 of the Tax Code for documents — 200 RUB per document not submitted).
